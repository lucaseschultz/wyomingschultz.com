Roman Numeral Standard Notation
  • Other than for subtraction, numerals must be ordered from largest (first) to smallest (last)
  • Numerals may be subtracted from by inserting the numeral to be subtracted before the numeral to be subtracted from (called the core numeral)
    • Only one numeral may be subtracted from the core numeral
    • The numeral that is subtracting from the core numeral must be the largest geometric numeral (1, 10, 100, etc.) smaller than the core numeral
